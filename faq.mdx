<Accordion trigger="Someone is impersonating me with a character. How do I stop them?">
While AI characters are fun to interact with and mimic real humans, some people use them to harm others and defame them. This is against the [Terms of Service](https://docs.aicordapp.com/documents/terms-of-service) of AICord and results in the server being banned or the removal of AI character.

**You can report impersonators to AICord Team on our support server, and we'll take action.**
</Accordion>
